Terms & Conditions
  • Incentives are released on the 15th of every month, after joinings + the client's maturity days.
  • If a candidate leaves before maturity days, the amount is adjusted in the next reimbursement of incentives/salary.
  • BD performance is calculated on the basis of clients signed within one year.
  • The client signed date is taken from the first selection date mentioned in the hired sheet.
  • BD incentives are paid post payment, on the next 15th of the month.
  • Maximum limit of ₹25,000 incentive per month, per employee.
  • The scheme is valid from 1 May 2026 to 31 March 2027.
  • In case of any changes, management reserves the discretion to make amendments.
