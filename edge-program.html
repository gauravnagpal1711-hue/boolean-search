Terms & Conditions
  1. The EDGE programme applies to all In-house KAMs and Franchise Recruiters actively working under GetWork for FY 2026–27 (April 2026 – March 2027).
  2. Performance is reviewed on a quarterly basis. Milestone rewards unlock only on achieving the qualifying credit target consistently for the specified consecutive months (3, 6, or 9 months).
  3. Fast Track Allowance earned under EDGE is treated as a permanent increment and added as a fixed bonus component of the recruiter's CTC. This is non-reversible once awarded at each milestone. This is a permanent increment as a fixed bonus part — awarded quarterly and carried forward.
  4. All benefits are cumulative. Higher milestone rewards include all prior milestone benefits.
  5. Credit classification (Flat / Mid band / Senior band) is based on the candidate CTC recorded in the Hired Sheet and invoiced.
  6. In case of candidate backout or exit before 90-day maturity at the client side, the joining credit and associated incentive are withheld or reversed in the following month's calculation.
  7. Franchise recruiters are eligible under the same credit and milestone structure. Payout attribution follows the standard franchise split policy (70% franchise / 30% in-house KAM).
  8. GetWork reserves the right to review and revise credit thresholds at the start of each new financial year. Mid-year revisions, if any, are communicated with 30 days' advance notice.
  9. Any disputes related to credit classification or milestone eligibility must be raised within 15 days of the relevant month's payout cycle. Disputes raised after this period will not be entertained.
  10. Participation is voluntary. However, once a recruiter qualifies for a milestone, the associated Fast Track Allowance is processed automatically in the following payroll cycle.
